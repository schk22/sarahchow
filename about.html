Her work has been covered by the Guardian, the Washington Post, Forbes, and DeSmog.


She lives in London, where she is a research consultant for the Oxford Climate Litigation Lab, a community organizer for Green Lions, and the occasional lunch club volunteer at the ESEA Community Centre.


She is directly influenced by the work and words of bell hooks, James Baldwin, Robin Wall Kimmerer, and Simone Weil.


On Friday mornings you can spot her earth tending in South London.
